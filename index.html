Story of Arrack

Over 100 years
of excellence
What begins as sap from the coconut flower “Toddy” is naturally fermented and distilled to perfection to produce pure coconut arrack, one of the most natural and organic beverages in the world. Coconut Arrack is unique & indigenous to Sri Lanka with its tropical, sunny flavours.
